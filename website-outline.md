# Cozy Coffee Website Outline

This document is a lightweight planning guide for launching a cozy informational website for a small-business coffee shop.

## Recommended Starting Stack

- Frontend: static-first site (HTML/CSS now, move to Next.js or Astro when needed)
- Hosting: Vercel or Netlify for simple drag-and-deploy workflows
- Forms: Formspree or Netlify Forms for contact form handling
- Analytics: Plausible or simple Google Analytics setup

Why this is a good fit:
- Low cost and low maintenance for V1
- Fast loading and easy to edit
- Focuses only on the essentials customers need to visit the shop

## Suggested Sitemap (Informational)

Launch now:
- `/` Home
- `/menu` Menu
- `/about` About
- `/visit` Visit Us
- `/contact` Contact

## Decisions to Make Before Launch

- Brand: final name, logo, colors, typography
- Content: menu items/prices, hours, address, social links
- Photography: in-shop shots, product photos, brand style
- Policy pages: privacy policy and terms (if needed)
- Local SEO: business description, map/listing links, location keywords

## Hosting and Launch Checklist

1. Buy or transfer domain name
2. Choose host and connect repository/folder
3. Point DNS records to host
4. Enable HTTPS certificate
5. Add page titles and meta descriptions
6. Add analytics
7. Test contact form delivery
8. Test mobile responsiveness
9. Run final proofread and link check
10. Publish and monitor

## Rough Cost Considerations

- Domain: around $10-$25/year
- Hosting: free tier to low monthly fee for small traffic
- Optional tools: form/analytics costs depending on provider and volume
- Optional tools: premium analytics/form tiers as traffic grows
